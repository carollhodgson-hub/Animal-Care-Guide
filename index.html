Daisy Care

Feeding time: once per day, midday between 12:00–3:00 p.m.

Food Instructions

  1. Use one prefilled bowl of Dr. Harvey’s oat mixture each day.
  2. Add 3 squirts of fish oil.
  3. Heat water in the teapot and mix it into the oat mixture until it gets really soft.
  4. Add 1/4 pack of Farmer’s Dog food from the refrigerator on top of the oat mixture.
  5. Each pack of Farmer’s Dog lasts 4 days. When the last portion runs out, get a new pack from the freezer and put it in a bowl in the refrigerator to thaw for the next day.

Going Outside

  • Try to let Daisy out every 2–3 hours, or when she gets up and when she goes to the bathroom and comes back give her a treat, you can break apart the larger ones to give to her after going to the bathroom and coming back inside when called.
  • Sometimes she needs to be prodded to go to the bathroom. Leave her out until she goes if needed.
  • She may run to the neighbor’s house if she sees people. If you go over there, she will usually want to come back with you. That is okay as long as you watch her and the neighbors are okay with it.
  • Use the small liver treats from the bowl to help her come to you. Shaking the treat bowl helps, especially if she goes after a squirrel.

Kennel

  • Daisy should not stay in the kennel more than 4–5 hours.
  • The kennel is under the turtle tank, on the left side.
  • Make sure there is a little water in the bowl on the side of the kennel.

Dog Water Fountains

  • Check dog water fountains to make sure there is enough water to run them.
  • Add water from refrigerator in white pitcher if they need water
  • There is a bowl in the kitchen and one upstairs in master bathroom beside showers

Clean-Up

  • Cleaner is beside the treats on the ice box chest next to the back door, you can use this to clean up accidents.
Important: Daisy is very friendly and usually does well with visitors, but she may run toward the neighbors if she sees people outside. Please keep an eye on her when outdoors.

Stray Cats

There are stray cats we feed. One had a collar and would come and go, and eventually kind of stayed, we call Fuzzy, but she stays outside.

  • Cat food is on the same chest where the treats and cleaner are kept.
  • You can put food in the bowl on the back porch or front porch.
  • Please make sure fresh water is available for the cats when possible, especially during hot weather.

    • Counter Ice Maker

    The counter ice maker may need water added.

    • It will say it needs water and will make a loud noise.

    • Use only filtered water from the refrigerator.

    BeeDee the Bearded Dragon

    Food

    • Give collard and other green mix from the big container with the yellow lid.
    • Add a few chopped carrot pieces and squash pieces.

    Poop / Cleaning

    Important: BeeDee’s poop needs to be cleaned promptly because she has had bacterial gut infections from feces particles getting transferred into water and food.
    • She will probably poop every day or every other day.
    • Clean up poop and anywhere it is smeared.
    • If poop is on BeeDee, wipe her with a warm paper towel.
    • If she needs a bath, call Caroll or Bryan first for help/instructions.

    Water / Fountain

    • Water should be okay in the fountain, but check to make sure there is still water and it has not leaked.

    Lights

    • During the day, two lights should be on.
    • At night, one infrared heat light should be on.
    • Day lights: turn on at 7:00 a.m. and off at 7:00 p.m.
    • Night light: turns on at 7:00 p.m. and off at 7:00 a.m.
    • If the lights need to be reset, push the circular button on the side of the Amazon smart plug.
    Smart plug reset reminder:
    Look for the circular button on the side of the Amazon smart plug. Press it to reset the lights if the schedule is not working.
    Very Important: BeeDee has previously developed bacterial infections from fecal contamination. Please clean any stool immediately and make sure food and water remain clean.

    Plants

    Plants will be watered before we leave and should be okay.

    Front Pond / Waterfall Filter

    If the fountain filter on the front pond under the waterfall gets really loud, add water. This generally means it needs more water and should be covered with water.

    1. Turn on the water from the back of the house under the kitchen window.
    2. Fill the pond until the filter is totally submerged.
